⚡ Pima County Scraper

Scrape Sheriff's Deeds from the Pima County Recorder and send them to our database.

Step 1: Open Pima Recorder Search

Open Search Page →

Solve the captcha and click "I Accept"

Step 2: Search for Sheriff's Deeds

Enter your search criteria and click Search. Wait for results to load.

Step 3: Drag this button to your bookmarks bar

⚡ Scrape to Oncor

Or right-click → "Add to Bookmarks" if you can't drag it

Step 4: Click the bookmark on the results page

After search results load, click the "⚡ Scrape to Oncor" bookmark. It'll grab all results and send them to the database tagged as Pima County.
